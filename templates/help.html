Frequently Asked Questions

  • How many leaves do I have?

    • Each faculty memeber has the following number of leaves:

    Teaching Staff

    1. Casual Leave: 15 days
    2. Earned Leave: 10 days
    3. Maternity Leave: 132 days
    4. Restricted Holiday :2 days

    Non Teaching Staff

    1. Casual Leave: 15 days
    2. Earned Leave: 10 days
    3. Maternity Leave: 132 days
    4. Restricted Holiday :2 days
  • What does leaves pending approval mean?

    • When you apply for a leave, It is under approval from your HoD, until it is approved or rejected, it's status is "Under Approval"
